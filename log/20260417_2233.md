## Decisions made

- **Removed both redundant title elements** (`section-tag` and
  `kc-label`) after two review passes. Two pieces of "Key Concept 2.X"
  labeling were competing; the purple callout with a bolded opening
  sentence is self-identifying.
- **Made the global "How to read this dashboard" card opt-in** in the
  skill, not default. It was added, then removed on review — the widget
  structure is already legible without it, and adding a dashboard
  preamble is a chapter-author choice.
- **Motivation uses pink accent (`--accent-3`)**, Key Concept uses
  purple (`--accent-2`), Try-this and take-away link use cyan
  (`--accent`). Role-based so chapters with different brand mappings
  still work.
- **Try-this steps always end with a reveal** (not a question). A step
  without an insight sentence is not finished.
