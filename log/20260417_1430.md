## New Chapters Built This Session

### Ch06 — The Least Squares Estimator (7 widgets)
Population vs. sample regression, OLS assumptions diagnostic, Monte Carlo unbiasedness simulator, SE anatomy, Gauss-Markov (OLS vs. alternatives), convergence clubs real-data sampling variability, SE vs. sample size.

### Ch07 — Statistical Inference for Bivariate Regression (7 widgets)
t-distribution explorer, confidence interval simulator with coverage rate, hypothesis testing framework, p-value explorer, economic vs. statistical significance, robust standard errors, house price full inference. Includes robust t-CDF implementation (regularized incomplete beta function).

### Ch08 — Case Studies for Bivariate Regression (7 widgets)
Health outcomes vs. expenditure, health vs. GDP (all/subset), outlier impact analysis, CAPM betas (KO/TGT/WMT), CAPM residual diagnostics, Okun's Law with prediction, Okun time series with subperiod regression.

### Ch09 — Models with Natural Logarithms (7 widgets)
Log approximation explorer, four model specifications (lin-lin/log-lin/log-log/lin-log), semi-elasticity visualizer, elasticity with returns classification, exponential growth & Rule of 72 with S&P 500, model comparison 2x2 grid, cross-country log models.

### Ch12 — Further Topics in Multiple Regression (6 widgets)
Robust SE selector (Default/HC1/HAC), prediction vs. confidence intervals, Type I/II errors & power, autocorrelation ACF, SE ratio comparator, bootstrap simulation.

### Ch16 — Checking the Model and Data (6 widgets)
VIF explorer, robust vs. standard SEs, autocorrelation explorer (AR(1) simulation), diagnostic plots, influential observations (DFITS/DFBETAS), omitted variable bias.
