### JavaScript math library

Implemented full t-distribution support:
- `lnGamma()` — Lanczos approximation
- `betaCF()`, `betaInc()` — Regularized incomplete beta function
- `tCDF()`, `tPDF()`, `tQuantile()` — Complete t-distribution
- `normalPDF()`, `normalCDF()` — Standard normal
